The Apocalypse Is a Business

A System Apocalypse LitRPG set in Turin, Italy.

The System arrived without warning. It classified, measured, and upgraded every human being on Earth — and gave them seventy-two hours to choose what they would become. Cecilia Leonelli, head of a Turin crime family, chose first and chose correctly. Now she is building something no other faction in the city understands yet: an organization so structurally necessary that its criminal nature becomes irrelevant.
